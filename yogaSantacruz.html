Name Description Recognition Opportunities
Yoga Therapy 100 hours Yoga Therapy course that offers specialized knowledge and therapeutic applications of yoga in 110 diseases, deepens the skill set of yoga teachers. Yoga Alliance Continuing Education (YACEP)
YCB - Level 6 - Yoga Therapist		 In-depth Expertise: Learn yogic management for 110 diseases with practical techniques and expert guidance.

Yoga for Cancer The 40 hours Yoga for Cancer focuses on specific methodologies for supporting individuals with cancer, which is a niche and advanced area of yoga teaching. Yoga Alliance Continuing Education (YACEP) Specialized Training: Become a certified cancer therapist and equip yourself to safely and effectively support cancer survivors with yoga practices tailored to their unique needs.
Advanced Pranayama Techniques The 40 hours Advanced Pranayama Techniques Deepens understanding and practice of pranayama, adding advanced breath work skills to a teacher's repertoire. Eligibility:

  • Yoga Therapy: Eligible for students who have completed 500-hour or 900-hour TTC, or equivalent certifications recognized by YCB level 2/3 or Yoga Alliance RYT 500. Medical Doctors who have completed any TTC (200/500/900 hrs.) from The Yoga Institute.
  • Yoga for Cancer: Eligible for students who have completed minimum 200 hours TTC from any Institution.
  • Advanced Pranayama Techniques: Open to all.
