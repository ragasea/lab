About The Academy

Welcome to the Mind Arena!

Chess Anurag Academy was founded by Anurag, a FIDE-rated player with over 15 years of competitive experience and a passion for teaching. Our mission is not just to teach the rules of chess, but to instill a deep love for the game and cultivate critical thinking, patience, and foresight in every student.

We believe that chess is more than just a game—it's a workout for the brain. Our curriculum is designed to be engaging, challenging, and most importantly, fun! We use a combination of classic teachings and modern technology to provide a comprehensive learning experience.
