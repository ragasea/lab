6. Importance of pH in Everyday Life

üåø Plants & Animals

Our body works within pH 7.0 to 7.8. Acid rain (pH < 5.6) makes river water acidic, endangering aquatic life.

ü¶∑ Tooth Decay

Starts when mouth pH < 5.5. Bacteria produce acid by degrading sugar. Toothpaste (basic) prevents decay.

üçî Digestion

Stomach produces HCl for digestion. Excess acid causes pain (Acidity). Antacids (Mg(OH)‚ÇÇ) neutralize it.

üêù Self Defense

Bee sting leaves Methanoic acid (pain). Nettle leaves inject methanoic acid. Dock plant (basic) is the remedy.
