About Us

Chess Anurag Academy is a premier chess training institute founded by GM Anurag Mohanty. We provide world-class chess training to students of all ages and skill levels.

Our mission is to nurture chess talent and help students achieve their full potential through systematic training, strategic guidance, and personalized coaching.

With over 20 years of experience in chess education, our academy has produced numerous national and international champions.
