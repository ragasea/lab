About Anurag Sharma

Government certified yoga instructor with over 20 years of personal practice (since 2001) and 13+ years of teaching experience (since 2011). Trained at prestigious institutions including Art of Living, Vipassana Center, Patanjali, Chandigarh College of Yoga, and Chandigarh Administration.

Experience includes teaching at government schools, private colleges in Kharar and Mohali, conducting weekly sessions at Chandigarh hospitals including PGI drug & alcohol de-addiction center, and corporate wellness programs for various MNCs.

Serves as Master Yoga Trainer for Chandigarh Administration, preparing participants for International Yoga Day events. Currently conducting regular sessions in residential societies of Kharar and surrounding areas.

Also passionate about chess coaching, helping students develop strategic thinking and mental acuity through this ancient game of kings.
