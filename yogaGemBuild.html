Anurag Sharma

Anurag Sharma is a government-certified yoga instructor with a passion for sharing the ancient wisdom of yoga. He has been practicing yoga and meditation since 2001 and has been teaching since 2011.

Anurag has learned from esteemed organizations such as the Art of Living, Vipassana Meditation Center, and Patanjali. His teaching style is a blend of traditional techniques and modern understanding, making yoga accessible for everyone.
