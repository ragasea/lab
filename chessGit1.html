About Us

Chess Anurag Academy is dedicated to teaching chess to students of all levels. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced player, our structured training programs, led by experienced coaches, will help you achieve your goals.

Our mission is to inspire and nurture the love for chess in every individual, fostering critical thinking and strategic planning skills that extend beyond the chessboard.

Meet Our Trainers

Our trainers include national and international chess champions with years of coaching experience. They are passionate about sharing their knowledge and helping students unlock their true potential.
