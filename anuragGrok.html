About Me

I am Anurag Sharma, a government-certified yoga instructor with over two decades of practice in yoga and meditation since 2001, and teaching experience since 2011. I have trained with renowned organizations including Art of Living, Vipassana Center, Patanjali, Chandigarh College of Yoga, and Chandigarh Administration.

My experience includes teaching at government schools, private colleges in Kharar and Mohali, weekly sessions at hospitals like PGI's drug and alcohol de-addiction center, corporate sessions for MNCs, and serving as Master Yoga Trainer for International Yoga Day events in Chandigarh.

In addition to yoga and meditation, I offer chess coaching to train minds and unlock potential, with India's best chess mentors guiding the sessions.
