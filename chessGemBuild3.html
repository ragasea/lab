About The Academy

Welcome to the Mind Arena!

Chess Anurag Academy was founded by Anurag Singh, a FIDE-rated player with over 15 years of competitive experience and a deep passion for teaching. Our mission is not just to teach the rules of chess, but to instill a love for the game while cultivating critical thinking, patience, and foresight in every student.

We believe that chess is more than just a game—it's a workout for the brain. We use a combination of classic teachings and modern technology to provide a comprehensive and fun learning experience for all age groups.
